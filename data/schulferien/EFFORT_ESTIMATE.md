## Recommendations

### Priority Order

1. **Complete ZH first** - Already started, methodology proven
2. **AR + AI next** - Small, quick wins (~2 hours total)
3. **SG** - Medium size, German-speaking (~4-5 hours)
4. **AG** - Larger but German-speaking (~12-16 hours)
5. **Standardized cantons** - Quick, official sources only (~2-3 hours)
6. **BE last** - Largest, bilingual complexity (~18-25 hours)

### Efficiency Tips

1. Batch by platform (Escola schools together)
2. Batch by language (DE, then FR, then IT)
3. Do standardized cantons as a break between complex ones
4. Use parallel sessions for independent Bezirke
