# TYPO3 Platform

## Overview

TYPO3 is an enterprise CMS used by some larger Swiss municipalities and schools.

## Identification

- `/fileadmin/` in asset URLs
- `/typo3/` in admin URLs
- Often more complex URL structures

## URL Patterns

### PDF URLs
```
/fileadmin/user_upload/[path]/[filename].pdf
Example: /fileadmin/user_upload/Bilder_und_Dokumente/0_Home/2_Downloads/Ferienplan_SJ_25-26_bis_SJ_32-33.pdf
```
