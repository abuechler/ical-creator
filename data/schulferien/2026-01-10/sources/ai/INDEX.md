## Administrative Structure

Appenzell Innerrhoden has 5 Bezirke (districts) which function as municipalities. These are divided into two regional groups with different holiday schedules:

### Innerer Landesteil (IL) - 4 Bezirke
- Appenzell (Hauptort)
- Schwende-Rüte
- Schlatt-Haslen
- Gonten

### Äusserer Landesteil / Bezirk Oberegg (BO) - 1 Bezirk
- Oberegg (forms two exclaves near St. Gallen)

## Holiday System

- **Structure**: Centralized with two regional variants
- **Planning Period**: Fixed for 3-year periods
- **Key Difference**: The two Landesteile have different dates for Herbstferien, Sportferien, and Sommerferien
- **Same Dates**: Weihnachtsferien and Frühlingsferien are typically the same for both regions
