# Kanton Aargau (AG) - School Holidays Source Index

**Collection Date:** 2026-01-11
**Status:** Phase 1 Complete (Cantonal Data)

## Overview

Kanton Aargau uses a **hybrid system**:
- **9 weeks cantonal uniform** (set by Erziehungsrat)
- **4 weeks local** (set by Gemeinden within framework)

## Local Holidays (4 weeks - varies by region)

### Sportferien

- **Duration:** Typically 1-2 weeks
- **Timing:** KW 5-9 (varies by Bezirk/Gemeinde)
- **Set by:** Gemeinden within departmental framework

### Additional Local Weeks

- Extended summer or autumn periods
- Varies by municipality

### Kompetenztage

Local school boards may declare up to 3 additional free days per year.
