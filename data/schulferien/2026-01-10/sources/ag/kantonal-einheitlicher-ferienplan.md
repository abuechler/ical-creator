## Coverage

This document covers **cantonal uniform holidays (9 weeks)** from school year 2024/25 through 2034/35.

### Cantonal Uniform Holidays

| Holiday | Duration | Calendar Weeks |
|---------|----------|----------------|
| Herbstferien (Autumn) | 2 weeks | KW 40/41 |
| Weihnachtsferien (Christmas) | 2 weeks | KW 52/01 (varies) |
| Fruhlingsferien (Spring) | 2 weeks | KW 15/16 |
| Sommerferien (Summer) | 3 weeks | KW 30/31/32 (varies) |

**Total cantonal uniform:** 9 weeks

### Sportferien Variation

The Sportferien (sport/winter holidays) are **NOT uniform** across the canton. Different regions/Bezirke may have different weeks within KW 5-9. This requires per-Bezirk or per-Gemeinde verification.

### Kompetenztage

Local school boards (Schulpflege) can declare up to 3 additional school-free days per year for:
- Local holidays
- Semester change
- Bridge days
- Can be split into half-days

## Special Notes from Document

1. **School year start:** Second Monday in August
2. **First semester end:** Between 3rd and 5th Saturday after New Year
3. **Date format:** First date = first holiday day (usually Monday), Second date = last holiday day (usually Friday)
4. **Maria Himmelfahrt regions:** In areas where Aug 15 is a public holiday, schools may start Wednesday instead of Monday when Aug 15 falls on Tuesday (applies to 2028 and 2034)
