**Retrieved:** 2026-01-11 01:33:00 CET

## Overview

Kanton St. Gallen uses a **hybrid system** for school holidays:
- **12 weeks** are set canton-wide by the Bildungsrat
- **1 week (Winterferien/Sportferien)** is set locally by each Schultrager (school authority)

This means cantonal data covers approximately 92% of all school holidays, with only minimal local variation.

## Holiday System

### Cantonal Uniform Holidays (set by Bildungsrat)

| Holiday | Calendar Weeks | Duration |
|---------|----------------|----------|
| Herbstferien (Autumn) | KW 40-42 | 3 weeks |
| Weihnachtsferien (Christmas) | 2 weeks | First Christmas day in first week |
| Fruhlingsferien (Spring) | KW 15-16 | 2 weeks |
| Sommerferien (Summer) | KW 28-32 | 5 weeks |

**Total cantonal:** ~12 weeks (fixed for all public Volksschulen and cantonal Mittelschulen)

### Local Holidays (set by Schultrager/Gemeinde)

| Holiday | Typical Timing | Notes |
|---------|----------------|-------|
| Winterferien/Sportferien | 1 week (varies KW 5-9) | **Only locally varying holiday** |
