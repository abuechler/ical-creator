## Content Overview

The document provides official holiday guidelines (Ferienrichtdaten) for all public schools in Kanton Appenzell Ausserrhoden for school years 2025-2028.

### Key System Notes

- The Departement Bildung und Kultur sets the annual school holidays
- Coordination with neighboring cantons is pursued
- **Local flexibility**: Each Gemeinde can independently set 2 of the 13 holiday weeks

### Sample Data (from research notes)

**School Year 2025/2026:**
- School start: Monday, August 11, 2025
- Herbstferien: Mo 06.10.2025 - Fr 17.10.2025 (2 weeks)
- Weihnachtsferien: Mo 22.12.2025 - Fr 02.01.2026 (2 weeks)
- Fruehlingsferien: Fr 03.04.2026 - Fr 17.04.2026 (2 weeks)
- Sommerferien: from Mo 06.07.2026
