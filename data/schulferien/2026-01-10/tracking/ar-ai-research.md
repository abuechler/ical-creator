# Appenzell Ausserrhoden (AR) and Appenzell Innerrhoden (AI) - School Holiday Research

**Research Date:** 2026-01-11

---

## Executive Summary

Both Appenzell cantons are small and have centralized holiday planning, making data collection relatively straightforward:
- **AR**: 20 Gemeinden with cantonal guidelines (Ferienrichtdaten) + 2 weeks local flexibility
- **AI**: 5 Bezirke with 2 distinct regional schedules (Innerer Landesteil vs. Oberegg)

**Estimated Collection Effort**: Low - central sources available for both cantons

### School Holiday System

**Structure**: Semi-centralized with local flexibility

**Key Characteristics**:
- The Department of Education and Culture (Departement Bildung und Kultur) sets annual holiday guidelines
- Coordination with neighboring cantons is pursued
- **Cantonal Ferienrichtdaten**: Official guidelines are published centrally
- **Local flexibility**: Each Gemeinde can independently set 2 of the 13 holiday weeks

**Quote from official source**:
> "Das Departement Bildung und Kultur legt die Schulferien jährlich fest. Dabei wird eine Koordination mit den Nachbarkantonen angestrebt."
> "Die Gemeinden haben die Möglichkeit zwei der 13 Ferienwochen selbstständig festzulegen."

## Kanton Appenzell Innerrhoden (AI)

### Administrative Structure
- **Number of Bezirke**: 5 (as of May 1, 2022, after Schwende-Rüte merger)
- **Note**: Bezirke function as the equivalent of Gemeinden in other cantons
- **Two Landesteile** (regional divisions):
  - Innerer Landesteil (4 Bezirke: Appenzell, Schwende-Rüte, Schlatt-Haslen, Gonten)
  - Äusserer Landesteil (1 Bezirk: Oberegg - forms two exclaves)

**Special Entity**: Feuerschaugemeinde (feuerschaugemeinde.ch) - handles building/fire police, water/energy for Appenzell village area

### School Holiday System

**Structure**: Centralized with two regional variants

**Key Characteristics**:
- Holiday schedule is fixed for three-year periods ("Der Ferienplan wird immer über einen Zeitraum von drei Schuljahren fixiert")
- **Two distinct schedules**:
  - **IL** (Innerer Landesteil inkl. Gymnasium Appenzell): Bezirke Appenzell, Schwende-Rüte, Schlatt-Haslen, Gonten
  - **BO** (Bezirk Oberegg): Separate dates for the exclave

**Quote from official source**:
> "Bitte beachten Sie die unterschiedlichen Ferienzeitpunkte im inneren und äusseren Landesteil."

## Suggested Approach for Data Collection

### Appenzell Ausserrhoden (AR)

**Recommended Strategy**: Hybrid approach

1. **Primary**: Collect cantonal Ferienrichtdaten from the official PDF
   - URL: https://ar.ch/.../FerienrichtdatenVolksschulenAppenzellAusserrhoden_v1.1.pdf
   - Provides the baseline for all 20 Gemeinden

2. **Secondary** (if needed for precision): Check individual Gemeinde websites for the 2 locally-determined weeks
   - Most Gemeinden likely follow the Richtdaten exactly
   - Consider sampling a few to verify consistency

**Effort Level**: LOW - One primary source covers ~85% of holidays, local variations are minor

### Appenzell Innerrhoden (AI)

**Recommended Strategy**: Centralized collection

1. **Primary**: Collect from cantonal Ferienplan page
   - URL: https://www.ai.ch/themen/bildung/eltern-und-schule/ferienplan
   - Covers all 5 Bezirke with just 2 schedule variants

2. **Data Structure**: Store as 2 regional schedules:
   - Innerer Landesteil (applies to: Appenzell, Schwende-Rüte, Schlatt-Haslen, Gonten)
   - Bezirk Oberegg (applies to: Oberegg only)

**Effort Level**: VERY LOW - Single source, 2 variants, covers entire canton

## Conclusion

Both Appenzell cantons are among the easiest in Switzerland for school holiday data collection:

1. **Appenzell Innerrhoden (AI)** is the simplest case - just 5 Bezirke with only 2 regional schedule variants, all from one central source.

2. **Appenzell Ausserrhoden (AR)** is nearly as simple - cantonal Richtdaten cover most holidays, with minor local variations for 2 weeks per Gemeinde.

**Recommended Priority**: Collect AI first (as proof of concept for simple cantons), then AR.

**Total Data Points**:
- AI: 2 schedule variants covering 5 Bezirke
- AR: 1-21 schedule variants (1 cantonal + potentially 20 Gemeinde-specific) covering 20 Gemeinden
