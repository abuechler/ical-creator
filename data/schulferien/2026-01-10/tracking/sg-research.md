# Kanton St. Gallen (SG) - Research Summary

**Research Date:** 2026-01-11
**Status:** Research Complete

## Overview

Kanton St. Gallen uses a **hybrid system** for school holidays: most holidays are set canton-wide by the Bildungsrat, but **Winterferien/Sportferien are set locally** by each Schultrager (school authority).

## Holiday System: Hybrid (Cantonal + Local)

### Cantonal Uniform Holidays

The **Bildungsrat** (Education Council) sets these uniformly for all public Volksschulen and cantonal Mittelschulen:

| Holiday | Duration/Weeks | Notes |
|---------|----------------|-------|
| Herbstferien (Autumn) | KW 40-42 (3 weeks) | Fixed |
| Weihnachtsferien (Christmas) | 2 weeks | First Xmas day in first week |
| Fruhlingsferien (Spring) | KW 15-16 (2 weeks) | Easter-based |
| Sommerferien (Summer) | KW 28-32 (5 weeks) | Fixed |

**Total cantonal weeks:** ~12 weeks

### Local Holidays

The **Schultrager (Gemeinde/Schulgemeinde)** sets:

| Holiday | Typical Timing | Notes |
|---------|----------------|-------|
| Winterferien/Sportferien | 1 week, varies (KW 5-9) | **Main variation** |

**Total local weeks:** 1 week

### Additional School-Free Days

- Auffahrtsbrucke (Friday after Ascension)
- Various religious holidays (Karfreitag, Ostermontag, Pfingstmontag)
- May 1 (afternoon)

## Key Differences from Other Cantons

| Aspect | St. Gallen (SG) | Zurich (ZH) | Aargau (AG) |
|--------|-----------------|-------------|-------------|
| Cantonal portion | 12 weeks (fixed) | 0 weeks | 9 weeks |
| Local portion | 1 week (Winterferien) | 13 weeks (all) | 4 weeks |
| Variation level | **Low** (only 1 week) | High | Medium |
| Data collection | Cantonal + Winterferien verification | Every Gemeinde | Cantonal + regional |

## Suggested Data Collection Approach

### Phase 1: Cantonal Data (15-30 min)

1. Download official PDF from sg.ch
2. Extract cantonal dates (12 weeks uniform)
3. Document as base template for all Gemeinden

### Phase 2: Winterferien Survey (2-3 hours)

1. Sample 1-2 schools per Wahlkreis (8-16 samples)
2. Identify Winterferien timing patterns
3. Check if Wahlkreise/regions have consistent patterns

### Phase 3: Full Winterferien Collection (if needed)

**Scenario A: Strong regional patterns**
- If Winterferien consistent within Wahlkreise
- Only need 8 regional variants
- Estimated time: 1-2 hours total

**Scenario B: Per-Gemeinde variation**
- If each Gemeinde differs significantly
- Need all 75 Gemeinden (or ~130 Schultrager)
- Estimated time: 5-8 hours

### Recommended Strategy

1. **Start with cantonal PDF** - covers 12/13 weeks
2. **Sample by Wahlkreis** - one school each to detect patterns
3. **Evaluate variation** - likely minimal (only 1 week varies)
4. **Priority sampling**: St. Gallen (capital), Wil (large), Rapperswil-Jona (urban)

## Data Collection Complexity

| Factor | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| Cantonal uniformity (12 weeks) | **Significantly reduces** effort |
| Only 1 local week | **Minimal** variation to track |
| 75 Gemeinden (vs 197 AG, 160 ZH) | **Moderate** if full collection needed |
| German only | **Simplifies** search terms |
| Wahlkreise have no admin function | Neutral (use for geographic grouping only) |

## Estimated Effort

| Approach | Entities | Time | Confidence |
|----------|----------|------|------------|
| Cantonal only | 1 | 15-30 min | High (covers 12/13 weeks) |
| Regional sample | 8 | 1-2 hours | High |
| Full (per Gemeinde) | 75 | 5-8 hours | Maximum |

**Recommendation:** Cantonal data plus regional sampling should be sufficient due to very limited local variation (only 1 week of Winterferien).

## Comparison with ZH

St. Gallen is **significantly easier** than Zurich for data collection:
- ZH: 100% local control = every Gemeinde needed
- SG: 92% cantonal + 8% local = mostly uniform
