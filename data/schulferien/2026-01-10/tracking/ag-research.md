## Holiday System: Hybrid (Cantonal + Local)

### Cantonal Uniform Holidays (9 weeks)

The **Erziehungsrat** (Education Council) sets these uniformly for the entire canton:

| Holiday | Duration |
|---------|----------|
| Herbstferien (Autumn) | 2 weeks |
| Weihnachtsferien (Christmas) | 2 weeks |
| Fruhlingsferien (Spring) | 2 weeks |
| Sommerferien (Summer) | 3 weeks |

**Legal basis:** SS 7 Abs. 1 Schulgesetz (SAR 401.100)

### Local Holidays (4 weeks)

The **Departement Bildung, Kultur und Sport** sets the framework after consulting Gemeinderaete.
Gemeinden set these 4 weeks within the framework, respecting:
- Traditional dates of their Bezirk
- Local circumstances

| Holiday | Typical Timing | Notes |
|---------|----------------|-------|
| Sportferien | KW 5-9 (varies!) | Most variation |
| Additional Sommer/Herbst | Varies | Extended summer or autumn |

**Legal basis:** SS 7 Abs. 2 Schulgesetz (SAR 401.100)

### Additional Local Days (Kompetenztage)

The **Schulpflege** (local school board) can declare up to 3 additional school-free days per year:
- Local holidays
- Semester change
- Bridge days
- Can be split into half-days

## Key Differences from Zurich

| Aspect | Zurich (ZH) | Aargau (AG) |
|--------|-------------|-------------|
| Cantonal portion | 0 weeks (all local) | 9 weeks (fixed) |
| Local portion | 13 weeks (all) | 4 weeks |
| Variation level | High (every Gemeinde) | Medium (mainly Sportferien) |
| Data collection | Every Gemeinde needed | Cantonal + verification |

## Suggested Data Collection Approach

### Phase 1: Cantonal Data (15-30 min)

1. Download official PDF from schulen-aargau.ch
2. Extract cantonal dates (9 weeks uniform)
3. Document as base template for all Gemeinden

### Phase 2: Regional Variation Survey (1-2 hours)

1. Sample 1-2 schools per Bezirk (11 samples)
2. Identify Sportferien timing for each region
3. Check if Bezirke have consistent patterns

### Phase 3: Full Collection (if needed)

**Scenario A: Strong Bezirk patterns**
- If Sportferien consistent within Bezirke
- Only need 11 regional variants
- Estimated time: 2-3 hours total

**Scenario B: Per-Gemeinde variation**
- If each Gemeinde differs significantly
- Need all 197 Gemeinden
- Estimated time: 12-16 hours (per EFFORT_ESTIMATE.md)

### Recommended Strategy

1. **Start with cantonal PDF** - covers 9/13 weeks
2. **Sample by Bezirk** - one school each to detect patterns
3. **Evaluate variation** - decide full vs. regional approach
4. **Priority Bezirke**: Baden (largest), Aarau (capital), Zofingen

## Data Collection Complexity

| Factor | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| Cantonal uniformity | **Reduces** effort (9 weeks fixed) |
| Only 4 local weeks | **Reduces** variation to track |
| Sportferien variation (KW 5-9) | **Main variable** to track |
| 197 Gemeinden | **High** if full collection needed |
| German only | **Simplifies** search terms |

## Estimated Effort

| Approach | Gemeinden | Time | Confidence |
|----------|-----------|------|------------|
| Cantonal only | 1 | 15-30 min | Low (misses local variation) |
| Regional (per Bezirk) | 11 | 2-3 hours | Medium |
| Full (per Gemeinde) | 197 | 12-16 hours | High |

**Recommendation:** Start with regional approach. Upgrade to full collection only if significant variation detected within Bezirke.
